Top 3 jobs

One person’s dream job may be another person’s nightmare. That said, certain jobs are universally coveted for reasons of salary, stability, status and intellectual challenge. Travel, creative outlets and a desire to help others are other factors that make a job enticing. The top 10 jobs in the world listed by various publications are in high demand and typically require a bachelor’s degree or higher.

U.S. News & World Report ranked information security analyst in the number one spot for best jobs in 2022. This career involves warding off cyberattacks and thwarting hackers attempting to breach a company’s confidential database. With a bachelor’s degree, the salary averages ​$103,590​ per year.

Coming in at second place on the U.S. News & World Report list of best jobs is advanced practice registered nurse. A nurse practitioner performs some of the same duties as a physician, such as developing treatment plans and prescribing medication. Helping patients live healthier lifestyles can be deeply rewarding. Salaries average ​$111,680​ per year.
